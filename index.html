About Us

Established in 2010, Fortynx School began as a small neighborhood school with a big vision: create a caring environment where every child discovers their strengths. Over the years we've grown into a vibrant community of learners, parents, and educators committed to excellence.

Our Vision

Nurture future leaders who are compassionate, curious, and confident.

Our Mission

Deliver high-quality education with focus on values and life skills.
